The Technical and Managment Fest

Born in 2009, with the soulful will of promoting technology, scientific thinking & innovation. Spark Quest has surpassed 11 years of creating an unmatched aura of a science & technology spectacle every year, at the campus of Techno India Hooghly at Chinsurah, West Bengal. In its aspiration to provide a platform to the student to portray their skill sets and talents in all kind of events. Spark Quest strives for one and all to get inspired & look up to.
